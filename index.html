Welcome to GrandFlora

offers a wide range of professional landscaping products and services which include provision of high grade lawn grasses, pots, trees, shrubs and ornamental plants, water features (swimming pools, fountains, ponds among others) Landscape architecture consultancy,land clearing, mowing and landscape maintenance

Our lead products and key specializations are in the propagation, establishment and maintenance of of ornamental utility lawn grasses. Establishment or implementation entails both hardscapes (walkways, parkings, garden sitting areas) and softscapes (lawn and plants) as well as interior design

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the number one landscaping company in Tanzania and east Africa at large offering high quality and professional landscaping services and products

Our Mission

The mission of Grandflora is to enhance architecture with nature, delighting our clients through a comprehensive range of innovative and professional gardening services, delivering with attention to detail, in a timely manner and at the most competitive cost possible.
